Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

TL;DR: We don't track you. Your data stays on your device. We only have your email for billing.

The Short Version

Death2Data is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. We've architected our system so that we physically cannot access most of your data, even if we wanted to.

What We Collect

Payment Information

When you subscribe, Stripe (our payment processor) collects:

  • Email address
  • Payment method (card details go directly to Stripe, we never see them)
  • Billing address (if required by your bank)

We receive only your email from Stripe, which we use to identify your subscription.

That's It

We do not collect:

  • Your name (unless Stripe provides it)
  • Your location
  • Your device information
  • Your browsing history
  • Your search queries
  • Your notebook entries
  • Analytics or tracking data

Where Your Data Lives

On Your Device (localStorage)

The following data is stored in your browser's localStorage:

  • Your membership token (just an expiration timestamp)
  • Your notebook entries
  • Your tool preferences

We cannot access this data. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

On Stripe's Servers

Stripe stores your payment information according to their Privacy Policy. This includes:

  • Email address
  • Payment history
  • Subscription status

On Our Servers

Nothing. We don't have a database of user data. We don't store your information.

Cookies

We don't use cookies. No tracking cookies. No analytics cookies. No cookies at all.

Third-Party Services

Stripe

We use Stripe for payment processing. When you pay, you interact directly with Stripe. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Netlify

Our site is hosted on Netlify. They may log IP addresses for security purposes. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Ollama (Optional)

If you use AI features, they run locally on your device via Ollama. No data is sent to external AI services.

Analytics

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts. We don't know how many people visit our site. We don't track what pages you view.

Data Export

Since your data is stored locally, you can export it anytime:

  • Open browser DevTools (F12)
  • Go to Application → Local Storage
  • Copy any data you want

Or use our export features within each tool.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Local data: Clear your browser's localStorage for death2data.com
  • Stripe data: Email us at matt@death2data.com and we'll request deletion from Stripe

Children

We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you're under 13, please don't use our services.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. For significant changes, we'll notify you via email.

Contact

Questions? Email matt@death2data.com
