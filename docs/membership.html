Membership

Last updated: February 2026

Pricing

$1 every 28 days. That's it. No hidden fees. No free tier (you get 3 free searches to try). Just $1 for a month of unlimited access to everything.

What's Included

Privacy Search

Unlimited searches. No tracking. No history. No surveillance. Search the web like you actually own your data.

28-Day Notebook

Pick a topic. Get daily prompts for 28 days. Write in your browser. Download your entries or get them printed at D2D events. All stored locally on your device.

QR Code Generator

Create QR codes for any URL, text, or contact info. SVG output. Print-ready. No tracking pixels embedded.

Analytics

Privacy-respecting analytics for your projects. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking. Just page views and metrics that matter.

Business Cards

Get custom business cards designed with your choice of brand. Available at select D2D events.

How to Join

Step 1: Visit the Site

Go to death2data.com. You'll see the entry gate.

Step 2: Click Through

Click the membership button. You'll be asked for your email.

Step 3: Enter Email

Give us your email. That's the only required information. We use it for billing and support.

Step 4: Payment

You'll be redirected to Stripe to enter your payment method. We never see your card number. Stripe handles all payment processing.

Step 5: Done

You're a member. Start using the tools immediately. No confirmation email needed. No waiting. Just use it.

Payment & Billing

How It Works

You pay $1 every 28 days. Your membership renews automatically unless you cancel.

Who Sees Your Card?

Not us. Stripe handles all payment processing. Stripe stores your payment method. We store your email and subscription status. Your card number never touches our servers.

Cancellation

Go to Account → enter your email → click "Open Billing Portal" → click "Cancel subscription". Takes 30 seconds. You keep access until your current billing period ends.

Why $1?

It Covers Our Costs

Stripe processing fees, domain registration, API hosting on Render. $1 lets us break even with a small user base and scale profitably as we grow.

It Aligns Incentives

If you pay, you're the customer. If it's free, you're the product. We chose customer. When your only income is $1/month per user, you can't afford to track them or sell their data. Your incentive is to be good.

It Respects Your Attention

$1 keeps the lights on without ads, sponsored content, or dark patterns. No notification spam. No upsells. Just tools that work.

No Free Tier

You get 3 free searches to try the tool. That's your trial. After that, membership is $1. We're not trying to trap you into a "free tier" that's actually a conversion funnel. It's $1. Period.

Refunds

No refunds. It's $1 - we'd spend more processing the refund than the refund itself. But you can cancel anytime and keep access until your current period ends.
