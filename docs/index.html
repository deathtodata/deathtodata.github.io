What is Death2Data?

Death2Data is a $1/month membership that gives you access to privacy-first tools. No tracking. No ads. No data harvesting.

We believe you should own your data, not rent it from companies who sell it to advertisers.

What's included?

  • Privacy Search - Search the web without being tracked
  • 28-Day Notebook - Daily AI prompts, physical notebook mailed at the end
  • QR Generator - Create QR codes for your projects
  • Business Cards - Digital-first business cards
  • Analytics - Privacy-respecting analytics for your sites

Quick start

  1. Pay $1 on the home page
  2. You're in. Access all tools immediately.
  3. Cancel anytime from Account

Philosophy

We don't track you. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. The $1/month covers our costs. That's the whole business model.

If you're the product, you're being exploited. At Death2Data, you're the customer.
