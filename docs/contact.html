Contact Us

How to Reach Us

Email

matt@death2data.com

That's It

No phone number. No chat widget. No ticket system. No Slack. No Discord community. Just email. A human reads it. A human responds.

Response Time

Usually within 24 hours. During weekends or holidays, might take a bit longer. But you'll get a response. I read every email and reply personally.

What to Include

For Bug Reports

What happened. What browser you're using. What device (desktop/mobile). Steps to reproduce if possible. Screenshots help.

For Billing Questions

Your email address. The charge date and amount. What your question is. Be specific about what's wrong or confused.

For Membership Help

What you're trying to do. What's stopping you. What you've already tried.

For Feature Requests

What you want. Why you want it. No guarantees we'll build it, but we read all suggestions.

Before You Email

Check the FAQ

Most common questions are answered here. Might save you time.

Check the Docs

Browse the documentation. We've documented everything we can.

Billing Issues?

Try the Stripe billing portal first. You can update payments, view invoices, and cancel from there without emailing us.

It's Probably Not Broken

If something isn't working, it might be browser cache, JavaScript disabled, or an extension blocking things. Try a different browser or incognito mode. That solves most "broken" reports.

What to Expect

Honest Answers

If we can't help, we'll tell you. If it's a limitation, we explain it. If it's a bug, we fix it or explain why we can't.

No Upsells

We're not going to pitch you a more expensive plan. We're $1/month. There's no more expensive plan.

No Data Collection

We don't ask for unnecessary information. We don't harvest your data. We don't track how many emails you send us.

No Nonsense

Direct answers. No marketing speak. No corporate jargon. Straight up.

Built By

Death2Data is built by calriven.com with AI-assisted development. Questions about development approach or tech stack? Email and ask.

Open Source

Some of our tools will be open sourced. Check the GitHub repo to see what's available. Want to contribute? Email us.
