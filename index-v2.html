analyzing stripe.com/atlas
Privacy Score
4 /10 — Mixed

Requires SSN, EIN, bank details. Shares data with banking partners. Standard for financial services but significant data exposure.

What You Should Know

Stripe Atlas collects extensive identity data to comply with KYC regulations. Your information is shared with their banking partner (Silicon Valley Bank) and payment processors. This is normal for incorporation services but means your personal data exists in multiple systems.

Ask Yourself
? Do I need a US incorporation, or would a local alternative reduce my data exposure?
? What happens to my data if I close my Stripe Atlas company?
? Have I read the data sharing agreement with their banking partner?
