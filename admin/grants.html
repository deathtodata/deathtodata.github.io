Grant Types (Simplified)

GOVERNMENT MONEY

  • SBIR/STTR: $50K - $2M, 6+ months, lots of paperwork
  • NSF: $50K - $500K, research funding
  • State Programs: $5K - $100K, varies by state

FOUNDATION MONEY

  • Tech Foundations (Mozilla, EFF, Knight): $10K - $200K
  • Local Foundations: $1K - $50K, community impact

TECH COMPANY PROGRAMS

  • Cloud Credits (AWS, Google, Azure): $1K - $100K in credits
  • Accelerators (YC, Techstars): $125K - $500K, 7-10% equity
