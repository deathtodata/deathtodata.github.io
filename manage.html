Common Questions

How do I cancel?

Click "Open Billing Portal" above, enter your email, click the link we send you, then click "Cancel subscription". You'll keep access until your current period ends.

How do I update my card?

Open the billing portal, then click "Update payment method". You can add a new card or remove old ones.

Where are my invoices?

All invoices are in the billing portal. You can download PDFs for your records.
